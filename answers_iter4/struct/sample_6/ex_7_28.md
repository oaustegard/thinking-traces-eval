The key insight is that unlike quartic theories with Z_2 symmetry, the cubic theory allows odd-parity corrections to the 2-point function; however, at order $g^1$, only tree-like (no-loop) corrections arise, which are already absorbed into the free propagator normalization. The first genuine loop correction appears at order $g^2$.
